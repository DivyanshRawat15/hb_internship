Console-Based Digital Clock & Lap Timer

Goal: Build a high-precision digital clock and lap-tracking system that exists entirely within the Browser Console.

Concepts: Recursive setTimeout, confirm().

    Requirements:
  • Utilize a recursive setTimeout pattern (not setInterval) to ensure synchronization with system time and prevent callback stacking.
  • Leverage the browser's confirm() dialog to act as a physical "Lap Button" trigger for handling user input and tracking lap times.
